Mission

The mission is simple: to bring The Word to those who need it most, through music they actually want to hear. Synthpop, vaporwave, and gospel truth — no bait-and-switch. If the music gets you in the door, Jesus keeps you there. Every beat, every lyric, every platform presence is an act of worship and outreach.

People

Who Are "We"?

Right now, it's just me — and the incredible people closest to me: my mom, my dad, and a handful of supportive colleagues who've walked this journey alongside me. Like anyone else, I have a past. But most importantly, I have a future — we all do, in Christ Jesus. I'm Born Again.

If you search for me online, you might find a photo or a snippet here and there from a former version of myself — someone careless, immature, and far from who I am today. No, I didn't scrub the internet clean or try to "future-proof" my reputation. My reputation isn't the point; The Word speaks for itself, and I'm just a vessel. That said, I did remove anything I felt could explicitly harm or mislead those young in faith or in years. My focus is on what matters: pointing to Jesus.

So, join me in dancing for the King of kings! I'm so thankful to be Saved — and I'm here to celebrate that with you.
